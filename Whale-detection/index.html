Case Study 01 — Regulated Crypto Casino

3,000 hidden whales. Six weeks. One million in deposits.

A large regulated operator had been investing heavily in VIP prediction using traditional data signals: deposit frequency, game activity, and CRM behaviour. The problem was that crypto players are inherently private. A player depositing 0.005 BTC looks identical to any other new registrant. The operator suspected significant high-value players were in their base, but had no way to prove it or act on it.

Approach

Whale Detection was applied across all BTC deposit wallets, both new registrations and existing players. Our clustering algorithm traced each deposit address to its related wallet network, calculating total on-chain net worth per player. Results were pushed directly into the operator's CRM, flagging which players warranted immediate VIP outreach via concierge, hosted events, on-site notifications, and personalised email flows.

Key results

  • Over 3,000 players identified as potential VIPs from the existing base
  • More than $1 million in deposit revenue generated within a six-week engagement window
  • Accumulated wallet balance across identified players exceeded 500 BTC
  • 30 players attained top-tier VIP status within the programme
  • 4 new players added directly to the hosted VIP programme

Case Study 02 — Hybrid Casino (Fiat + Crypto)

Crypto went from 15% of revenue to 35% in six months.

A hybrid operator with a well-established fiat player base had been accepting crypto payments for two years, but crypto deposits had plateaued at around 15% of total revenue share. The team assumed their crypto audience was simply smaller in size. What they had not considered was that their crypto players were chronically under-engaged — treated identically to fiat players despite holding vastly different wealth profiles on the blockchain.

Approach

Whale Detection was run across the operator's full crypto deposit history, starting from the oldest wallets in the database. This surfaced a segment of players with significant on-chain holdings who had been receiving generic communications and standard bonus offers. A dedicated crypto VIP tier was created, with personalised incentives, higher limits, and a concierge team specifically briefed on the profile of each identified player.

Key results

  • Crypto revenue share grew from 15% to 35% of total platform GGR within six months
  • Average crypto deposit value increased by 3.8× among the identified segment
  • Churn among the flagged high-value players dropped by 41% after personalised outreach began
  • The operator launched a dedicated crypto VIP tier that did not exist before the engagement
  • Return on the intelligence investment was achieved within the first four weeks of activation
